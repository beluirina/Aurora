Once Aurora (En gang Aurora)

52min | Documentary, Music | 9 December 2018 | Norway

Once Aurora is AURORA's 3rd documentary and her first full-length documentary. It was filmed during various shows in 2016, 2017 and 2018 and in La Fabrique, France while AURORA was producing her second album. The film has two versions, one for film festivals and one for TV/streaming. On April 5, 2018 a little teaser was published, titled as "Our Queendom Come".

Nothing is Eternal

10min | Documentary, Short | 4 August 2016 | USA

Nothing Is Eternal is the 2nd mini-documentary AURORA filmed.

In the beginning, AURORA explains the reason why she's making it, saying "I’m making this video because I’m afraid of what might happen. I might not be here tomorrow or the day after, so I want to make this video to keep the things that inspire me alive.”

Into the Light

5min | Documentary, Music | December 14, 2015 | Bergen

Into the Light is a mini-documentary that AURORA filmed for the announcement of her debut album All My Demons Greeting Me as a Friend. It was directed by Greg Kotler (Kasafilms) at AURORA's home in Bergen.
